Scenario: Improving Rider Trip Acceptance Rate (CTR) with a New Upfront Fare Display

Uber wants to test a new upfront fare display design on the rider app.
The new design shows:
Estimated fare
Estimated pickup time
Expected driver rating
A confidence interval of price (e.g., “fare may vary between ₹210–240”)
The hypothesis is that more transparency = higher trip acceptance rate.

You are asked to design an A/B test.
